# Switches

Enterprise campus / access switches and data-center switches.

## Selection criteria

- Vendor positioned as a Leader in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for
  Enterprise Wired and Wireless LAN Infrastructure, or in the equivalent
  Data Center Switching evaluations.
- Product line currently shipping and refreshed within the last 3 years.
- Enterprise / data-center targeting; ISP aggregation and metro switches
  are excluded.

## In-scope vendors and product lines

| Vendor           | Campus / access                | Data center                  |
|------------------|--------------------------------|------------------------------|
| Cisco            | Catalyst 9200 / 9300 / 9400 / 9500 | Nexus 9000 (9300, 9500)  |
| Arista Networks  | CCS 720XP (campus)             | 7050X, 7060X, 7280R, 7800R   |
| Juniper Networks | EX4400 / EX4100                | QFX5000 / QFX10000           |
| HPE Aruba        | CX 6000 / 6300 / 6400          | CX 8000 / 9300 / 10000       |

## Out of scope

- Carrier-grade aggregation platforms (Cisco NCS, Nokia 7250 IXR).
