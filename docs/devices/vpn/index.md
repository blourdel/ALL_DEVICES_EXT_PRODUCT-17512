# VPN

Remote-access and site-to-site VPN gateways. Most modern VPN
functionality is integrated into NGFWs or delivered as ZTNA from an SSE
platform; both shapes are covered here.

## Selection criteria

- Vendor positioned as a Leader in Gartner Magic Quadrants for Network
  Firewalls or Security Service Edge (SSE / ZTNA).
- Product line shipping and updated within the last 3 years.
- Enterprise remote-access or site-to-site use cases.

## Out of scope

- Consumer VPN services.
- ISP-managed MPLS / L3VPN provider edge devices.
