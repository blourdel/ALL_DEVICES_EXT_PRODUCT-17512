# Routers

Enterprise routers used at branch, campus and data-center WAN edges.
Service-provider / carrier-grade core routers are out of scope per the
exclusion criteria in [index.md](../index.md).

## Selection criteria

- Vendor positioned as a Leader in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for
  Enterprise WAN Edge Infrastructure (or its current equivalent).
- Product line actively sold and updated within the last 3 years.
- Targets enterprise (branch / campus / DC edge), not ISP backbone.

## In-scope vendors and product lines

| Vendor              | Recent product lines                                  |
|---------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| Cisco               | Catalyst 8000 Edge Platforms, ISR 1000 / 4000         |
| Juniper Networks    | MX (enterprise SKUs), ACX (enterprise), SRX as router |
| HPE Aruba           | CX 9300, CX 10000 (routing/DPU)                       |
| Fortinet            | FortiGate (SD-WAN / routing modes)                    |
| Huawei (enterprise) | NetEngine AR series                                   |

## Out of scope

- Cisco ASR 9000 / NCS, Juniper PTX, Nokia 7750 SR, Arista 7800R — these
  are ISP / carrier backbone platforms.
